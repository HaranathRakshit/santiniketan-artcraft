About Santiniketan

Discover the cultural heritage and artistic traditions of this historic town

The Cultural Heart of Bengal

Santiniketan, meaning "Abode of Peace," is a small town in West Bengal, India, that has been a center of art, culture, and education for over a century. Founded by Nobel laureate Rabindranath Tagore, this town has nurtured countless artists, craftspeople, and cultural traditions.

Traditional Crafts

The region is renowned for its traditional crafts including pottery, weaving, bamboo work, leather crafting, and folk art. These crafts have been passed down through generations, preserving the rich cultural heritage of Bengal.

Our Mission

Santiniketan Artcraft is dedicated to preserving these traditional crafts and supporting the artisans who create them. We connect skilled craftspeople with global markets, ensuring fair wages and sustainable livelihoods.
